Tapa King
Street, City, Municipality, Country
Tapa King reigns supreme with its mouthwatering array of Filipino comfort food, centered around its iconic beef tapa. Tender, marinated beef slices are served alongside garlic rice and a choice of egg, creating a hearty and satisfying meal. With each bite, Tapa King delivers a taste of Filipino home-cooked goodness that keeps customers coming back for more.
