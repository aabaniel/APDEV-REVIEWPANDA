Mang Inasal
Street, City, Municipality, Country
Mang Inasal's food is a celebration of Filipino flavors, grilling up deliciously juicy and flavorful chicken and pork barbecues marinated in traditional local spices. Complemented by unlimited rice and savory condiments like their signature chicken oil, every meal is a feast that captures the essence of Filipino street food culture with each mouthwatering bite.
